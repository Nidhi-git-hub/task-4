Why Stenna?

We bring you the most eco-friendly solution to beautifying your home. We go the extra mile to ensure that our wallpapers aren’t just healthy for your family, but also for the environment.

By using only water-based ink for both production and application, we radically reduce toxic emissions, improving the health of our production sites, and creating an eco-friendly, chemical-free environment in your home.

Our wallpapers are applied using high-quality adhesives that are specially crafted for Indian walls.
